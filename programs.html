Our Youth Empowerment program is dedicated to providing educational resources, tutoring, and mentorship to young people in our community. We believe that investing in our youth is one of the most impactful ways to create lasting positive change.

Our Activities:

After-school tutoring sessions
College and career guidance
Leadership development workshops
Scholarship opportunities
Skills training programs
Taekwondo martial arts classes for all age groups and levels

Impact:

Since launching this program, we've helped over 500 young people improve their academic performance, with 85% of our participants going on to pursue higher education or enter skilled employment.

The Community Wellness program focuses on promoting physical and mental health for all ages through educational workshops, health screenings, and community activities. We believe that a healthy community is a thriving community.

Our Activities:

Free health screening clinics
Mental health awareness workshops
Senior wellness programs
Nutrition and cooking classes
Exercise and fitness groups

üìà Economic Uplift

Our Economic Uplift program provides skills training and financial literacy workshops to support sustainable livelihoods. We focus on empowering individuals with practical tools and knowledge needed to achieve economic stability.

Our Activities:

Vocational skills training
Financial literacy workshops
Small business development support
Craft and artisan training
Microfinance opportunities

Impact:

Through this program, over 300 individuals have gained new skills that led to employment or started their own businesses, contributing to the economic growth of our community.
